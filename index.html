Why Mining Stocks in 2026?

Three structural tailwinds are converging to create a generational opportunity.

Copper Supercycle

The energy transition requires 2-3x more copper than the world currently produces. EVs, solar, grid infrastructure — all copper-intensive. Supply deficit projected through 2035+.

Gold at All-Time Highs

Central bank buying, de-dollarization, and inflation hedging have pushed gold above $5,000/oz. Producers with low AISC are printing cash — and many are still undervalued.

Massive Valuation Gap

Mining stocks are trading at 3-6x EBITDA while tech trades at 20-30x. Many miners have zero debt, strong cash flows, and trade below their net asset value.
