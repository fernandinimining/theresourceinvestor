About The Resource Investor

Who we are, why we publish, and how we stay transparent with readers.

About Us

The Resource Investor is an independent research platform helping investors navigate the mining and resource sector. Our team combines Wall Street experience with deep mining industry knowledge—covering geology, project economics, jurisdictions, and public-market valuation—so you can evaluate opportunities with clearer context.

We focus on education and analysis: how copper and gold cycles work, what to look for in producers and developers, and which tools can help you screen and track mining stocks. Our writers and editors have spent years following commodity markets, equity research, and on-the-ground developments in major mining regions. That experience informs how we frame risks, catalysts, and the limits of any single article or data point.

Expertise you can verify: We cite sources where practical, explain methodology in plain language, and separate opinion from fact. When we highlight a broker, screener, or service, we explain why it fits resource investors—not just that we like the brand.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make high-quality resource investing research accessible: clear guides, honest trade-offs, and tools that match how retail and semi-professional investors actually work. We aim to help you ask better questions—about reserves, costs, balance sheets, and geopolitical risk—whether you are new to mining stocks or refining an existing process.

We are not a registered investment adviser, broker-dealer, or financial planner. We publish information and education; any decision to buy, sell, or hold securities is yours alone, ideally with advice from a professional who knows your situation.
